Questions we hear often

Where does Astra gather research data?

Astra synthesizes information from public academic databases (Google Scholar, arXiv, PubMed), grant repositories, lab and company research pages, and other open web sources. Private data is only used when explicitly provided by the user or institution.

Can Astra adapt to my experience level?

Yes. During onboarding we ask about your background and confidence with research methods. The briefings and learning plans adjust terminology, depth, and pacing to match.

Is outreach messaging customizable?

You can tweak tone, attach your resume or portfolio, and flag specific projects you want to reference. Astra suggests language grounded in the professor‚Äôs current work.

How does Astra stay up to date?

Automated refresh cycles keep briefs current by re-scraping new publications, grants, and lab updates. You receive notifications when meaningful changes occur.
