Key Feature

Research mapping & discovery

Astra builds a live knowledge graph across publications, grants, patents, and lab sites so students can see the landscape before they ever reach out.

What it does

We pull structured data from academic and industry sources, normalize author, institution, and keyword metadata, and surface the most influential work in the field you care about.

With interactive clustering, Astra reveals emerging directions, keeps tabs on grant movements, and shows you which questions are heating upâ€”no manual digging required.

Snapshot outputs

  • Top papers
    Auto-curated reading stack

    Ranked by influence, recency, and overlap with your stated interests.

  • Connections
    Topic clusters

    Visual groupings help you understand how subfields relate in minutes.

  • Signals
    Grant and funding momentum

    See which labs and sponsors are investing in the space right now.

Inputs

  • Research area or professor of interest
  • Existing coursework or projects to prioritize sources
  • Preferred depth (survey vs. deep dive)
